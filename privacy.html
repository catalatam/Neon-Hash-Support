Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect personal data. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We use AdMob for optional reward ads. That's it.

Information We Collect

Neon Hash is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Local Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Purchased generators and upgrades
  • Achievement unlocks
  • Settings preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Advertising

Neon Hash uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded video ads. When you choose to watch an ad for a reward:

  • AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad serving
  • On iOS, we request ATT (App Tracking Transparency) permission to show personalized ads
  • You can decline ATT and still receive non-personalized ads
  • All ads are optional - you are never forced to watch ads

For more information about AdMob's data practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

No Account Required

Neon Hash does not require account creation, login, or any form of user authentication. You can play the entire game without providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Neon Hash is rated for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not contain any social features, chat, or user-generated content.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device using secure device storage APIs, your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device encryption features when available
  • Enabling device passcode/biometric locks

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: ogulcan.catalatam@gmail.com

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. All game data is under your control on your device. You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app.
